1. Introduction to Rheology

1.1 What is Rheology?

Rheology is the science that deals with the way materials deform when forces are applied to them. (재료에 힘이 가해졌을 때 재료가 어떻게 변형되는지)
So the two key terms of rehology are deformation and force.

Two principal aspects of rheology.
One involves the development of quantitative relationships between deformation and force for a material of interest.
