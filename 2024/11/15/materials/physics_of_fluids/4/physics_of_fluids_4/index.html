4.2 Thermodynamics of Microcanonical Ensemble

A microcanonical ensemble is closed system. The variables that uniquely describe the state of the system are number of particles N, volume V, energy E. (열과 일을 환경과 교환 X)

The state function is entropy, S = S(N, V, E)

First and Second Laws of Thermodynamics Combined

T: Temperature
p: Pressure

We have used the relation for quasi-static mechanical work dW - pdV and also considered a chemical work of particle insertion .

Total Differential of Entropy (S):

$$dS = \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial E}\right){N, V} dE + \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial V}\right){E, N} dV + \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial N}\right)_{E, V} dN$$

By comparing the dS, dE we identify the relations
$$\frac{1}{T} = \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial E}\right){N, V}, \quad \frac{p}{T} = \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial V}\right){E, N}, \quad -\frac{\mu}{T} = \left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial N}\right)_{E, V}$$

By comparing dE, dS (E = E(S, V, N)), we obtain
$$T = \left(\frac{\partial E}{\partial S}\right){N, V}, \quad -p = \left(\frac{\partial E}{\partial V}\right){S, N}, \quad \mu = \left(\frac{\partial E}{\partial N}\right)_{S, V}$$
